Boxborough, MA

Boxborough is a small town about 30 miles west of Boston. The town is about 10 sq mi and has a ppulation of about 5,000 people. The town itself is small and quiet, with only 3 stop lights in the town, all within a mile of eachother. There aren't many attractions in the town but the many conservation areas. However, the town itself has a rich with Revolutionary War history as the founders of the town had a part at the battle of Lexington and Concord.
